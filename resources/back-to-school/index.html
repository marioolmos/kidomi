This week we want to help kids to develop the ability to think clearly and rationally. With this they will be able to make logical connections between ideas.

Unit 4: Time to Read

Understanding the written word is one way the mind grows in its ability. Teaching young children to read helps them develop their language skills. It also helps them learn to listen.
