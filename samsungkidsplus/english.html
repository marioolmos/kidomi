Dear Samsung Kids+ Customer,

We have made the difficult decision to end the Samsung Kids+ subscription service, effective Sunday, February 20, 2021. It has been our pleasure to serve our Samsung Kids+ families over the past five years, bringing thousands of children the joy of fun and learning.

Samsung Kids+ was brought to you through a strategic partnership with Fingerprint, the leading provider of premium content services for children. Fingerprint operates an award winning kids content service called Kidomi, which provides everything you love about Samsung Kids+ – the best learning and entertainment content in a 100% safe environment with no advertisements, in-app purchases, or external links as well as parent tools to customize your child’s experience.

As a special thank you, we are providing 3 months free (a $15 value)* of Kidomi exclusively to Samsung Kids+ customers.
Learn More About Kidomi
Over the coming weeks we will be reaching out with instructions on how to transition Samsung Kids+ before the shutdown date on February 20, 2021. For more information about cancellations, please visit https://samsungkidsplus.com/transition

We thank you again for your support of Samsung Kids+. We greatly appreciate your business.

Sincerely,
The Samsung Kids+ Team
